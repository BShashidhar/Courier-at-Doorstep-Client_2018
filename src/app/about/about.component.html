About Courier@DoorStepclose
We are providing the srvices which are designed for the customer's time-sensitive needs.

The Value Added Services are introduced so that the customer needs are fulfilled

Value Added Service (VAS) includes the following:

  1. Collect on Delivery (C.O.D.): For this product, the "invoice value" of the item dispatched is collected from the consignee at the time of delivery.
  2. To-Pay or Freight on Delivery (F.O.D.): For this product, the "courier or freight charges" of the consignment are collected from the consignee at the time of delivery.

Intracity includes the facility of pick-up and delivery in the same city. It is custom-built for the distribution of:
  1. Bulk documents such as mobile or insurance bills, bank statements and annual general reports.
  2. Pamphlets, direct mailers, invitation cards and so on.
